About ABSO

What is Texas ABSO?

The Ahlul Bayt Student Organization is a student-led organization that aims to provide social growth and spiritual development opportunities to Shia Muslim youth at the University of Texas at Austin. Through lectures, prayers, supplications, social affairs, community service, and spreading awareness of the teachings of the Prophet (SAW) and Ahlul Bayt (AS), we establish a foundation that fosters growth and success.

Why should you join ABSO?

UT Austin is thrilled to be hosting ABSO for 5 years now! With over 100 members, students here enjoy a variety of benefits from guest speakers, religious activities, social gatherings, and weekly supplications. We welcome anyone who is interested in joining our small family to acquire a solid foundation of the teachings of the Prophet Muhammed (SAW) and his pure family the Ahlul Bayt (AS).

When do we have our meetings?

We don't have weekly required meetings, however, we will be hosting supplication (duas) activities every week. Join our social media for more updates.

Purpose

The purpose of the ABSO is to foster a supportive community for Muslim students on campus and promote understanding of Islamic culture and values.

Vision

Our vision is to create an inclusive environment that celebrates diversity, educates about Islam, and encourages meaningful interfaith dialogue.
